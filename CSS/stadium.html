Stadium High School

Stadium is one of the most beautiful schools in the world. It is an enormous castle that used to be a hotel. Ten things I Hate about you was filmed here.

Mr. Ikeda

Mr. Ikeda is the principal of our school. He is always working with the students and coming into our classrooms. He is also very good at impersonating Elvis and doing magic tricks.

Ms. Sage

Ms. Sage is a vice principal here at Stadium. She helps many students and helps carry out the school guidelines.

S. Marshall

Mrs. Marshall used to be my Middle School vice principal, but later became the vice principal of Stadium. She always tries to help out students but keeps a strict dress code.

D. Folsom

Mr. Folsom has two kids that go to this school. He is also the vice principal here at Stadium and keeps students in line.

Favorite Sport

Soccer and basketball are the most popular here at Stadium. More people seem to go to the basketball games, but I enjoy watching the soccer games.
